Sector 01

Mining & Heavy Engineering

Ghana's mining sector operates at the intersection of extreme physical conditions and high-value production. Downtime is not measured in hours — it's measured in millions of cedis.

Our proximity to Tarkwa — Ghana's mining capital — means we understand the operational realities: post-blast protocols, underground communications, fleet coordination, and the constant pressure to reduce Lost Production Time.

From deploying fibre across active mine sites to building the Sentry Nodes that will eventually replace humans in post-blast re-entry assessments, we engineer for the most unforgiving environment on the planet.

Automated sensor networks measure CO, NOx, and other blast gases to clear re-entry faster and safely.
🚁
Sentry UAV Reconnaissance
Autonomous aerial units survey post-blast zones before any human re-entry — reducing LPT by hours.

Sector 03

Legal & Professional Services

Legal firms handle the most sensitive documents that exist in any economy — land deeds, contracts, client testimony, and proprietary agreements. These cannot be stored on a generic cloud platform.

Our Radiant Vault solution gives legal firms an encrypted, sovereign document repository that keeps sensitive files entirely under the firm's control — with role-based access, full audit trails, and zero external cloud dependency.
