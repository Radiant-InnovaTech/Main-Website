Autonomous orchestration

A constellation of specialised AI agents

One reasoning core coordinates seven dedicated agents, each an expert in its domain, each carrying its own confidence score, all sharing a single operational memory.

One core, seven specialists.

The reasoning core is the strategic layer above the agent constellation. It doesn't just route signals — it interprets context, weighs evidence chains, and decides when to act autonomously versus when a human decision is required.

Plans
Decomposes incoming alerts into structured sub-tasks delegated to the appropriate specialist agents.
Weighs
Aggregates confidence scores from all seven agents before endorsing any action or escalation.
Remembers
Writes structured observations to persistent operational memory — every pattern, every outcome, retained across sessions.
Gates
Enforces supervised action boundaries — no containment runs without analyst approval above the configured confidence threshold.

How they work together.

A single investigation flows through all seven agents in sequence, each handing context to the next.

1
Detection STREAMING

Normalised telemetry crosses a behavioural threshold. Raw event is elevated to a structured alert object with an initial confidence score.

2
Correlation CLUSTERING

Related alerts across time and entities are clustered into a unified incident. 150 alerts collapse to 1 investigation thread.

3
Investigation REASONING

A kill-chain timeline is assembled. Lateral movement paths are traced. Entities are linked with 97.3% trace confidence and evidence provenance.

4
Threat Intel ENRICHING

All observables are enriched against 52 live feeds. TTPs are mapped to MITRE ATT&CK. Threat actor attribution is appended to the investigation graph.

5
Response HUMAN GATE

Containment playbooks are staged with rollback verification. No action executes without explicit analyst approval. Three actions await gate clearance.

6
Reporting AUDIT READY

A tamper-evident incident report with full action provenance is auto-generated. Compliance artefacts for ISO 27001, SOC 2, and GDPR are attached.

7
Learning ADAPTING

Analyst decisions, corrections, and outcomes are written to persistent memory. Detection thresholds are recalibrated. The constellation gets sharper with every case.
