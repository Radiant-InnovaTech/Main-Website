Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before talking to the team.

How is pricing determined?

Pricing is based on event ingestion volume, number of environments managed, deployment model, and support tier. We scope each engagement to your actual footprint — no inflated baseline costs.

Can I test before committing?

Yes. We offer a structured 30-day pilot against your live telemetry, with onboarding support and clear success criteria defined upfront. No credit card required during the pilot period.

Does on-premise cost more?

On-premise deployment is available at the Enterprise tier and reflects the additional infrastructure and deployment engineering involved. Cloud SaaS deployments are typically faster and lower cost to start.

What integrations are included?

The Professional and Enterprise tiers include unlimited integrations. We maintain native connectors for 40+ security tools including Splunk, CrowdStrike, Okta, Microsoft Sentinel, Palo Alto, and more.

How long does deployment take?

Cloud SaaS deployments can be live in as little as 48 hours once data connectors are configured. On-premise deployments typically take 2–3 weeks including environment preparation and agent tuning.

Is there a minimum commitment?

Starter pilots are month-to-month. Professional tier agreements are typically annual with discounts available for multi-year commitments. Enterprise is structured per custom negotiation.

Ready to talk numbers?

Our team will scope a pilot that fits your environment and budget within one call.
