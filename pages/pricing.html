FAQ

Common Questions

How is pricing determined?

Pricing is based on event ingestion volume, number of environments managed, deployment model, and support tier. We scope every engagement to your actual usage — no paying for capacity you don't use.

Can I test before committing?

Yes. We offer a structured 30-day pilot program where we deploy Nexus against your real environment with full support. You see the value before signing any contract.

Does on-premise cost more?

On-premise deployment is available at the Enterprise tier. It involves additional infrastructure planning and onboarding time, but the AI capabilities are identical to the cloud version.

What integrations are included?

The Professional and Enterprise tiers include unlimited integrations. We maintain native connectors for 40+ security tools and support any system with a REST API or webhook capability.

How long does deployment take?

Cloud SaaS: as fast as 48 hours for initial data ingestion and first detections. On-premise: typically 2–3 weeks including infrastructure setup, integration wiring, and baseline training.

Is there a minimum commitment?

Starter pilots are month-to-month. Professional tier is typically annual. Enterprise contracts are multi-year with custom terms. We work with your procurement process.

Ready to Talk Numbers?

Every deployment is scoped individually. Tell us about your environment and we will build a proposal that fits your budget and requirements.
