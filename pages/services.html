In Active Development
The Sentry Project

Autonomous Land & Air Sentry Nodes


We are currently engineering autonomous Land and Air Sentry Nodes for post-blast re-entry and underground reconnaissance in Ghana's mining sector.

After a blast, mines face hours of Lost Production Time (LPT) waiting for gas levels to clear before humans can re-enter. Our Sentry Nodes go in first — mapping the environment, measuring gas concentrations, and transmitting live data to surface teams.

The result: dramatically reduced LPT, zero human risk during re-entry assessment, and real-time underground situational awareness.
