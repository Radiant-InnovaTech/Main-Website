OUR THESIS

Solving the human scalability limit.

Radiant Nexus is the autonomous SOC platform built by Radiant InnovaTech — an engineering firm in Tarkwa, Ghana. We replace decorative alert screens with verifiable, machine-speed investigation loops.

"Adding more analytics to an overwhelmed SOC doesn't create security — it creates operational paralysis. Protecting modern infrastructure requires autonomous, explainable machine orchestration."

For a decade, security operations have been buried under the weight of their own tooling. SIEMs collect terabytes of telemetry daily and run thousands of static rules that trigger endless alert explosions — and Tier-1 analysts spend their days as human copy-paste bridges between detection tools, lookup tables, and ticket queues.

The problem isn't that activity goes unrecorded. It's that people can't reason through raw data streams at machine speed. Containment timelines have lengthened, not shortened, even as logging has improved.

Our engineering approach

Radiant InnovaTech builds multi-agent operational systems, not chatbots bolted onto a data lake. We abstract the functional roles of a real SOC team — detection, correlation, investigation, threat intel, response, reporting — into specialised software agents that assemble relational graphs and verify hypotheses autonomously.

Supervised autonomy keeps humans as the executive checkpoint on irreversible containment, while low-risk Tier-1 validation runs instantly. Analysts move from reactive alert janitors to proactive tactical commanders.
