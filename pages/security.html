Security & trust

Autonomous, but accountable.

Nexus investigates on its own, and explains every step. Humans hold the keys to anything that changes your environment — every action is gated, signed, and reversible.

HUMAN-IN-THE-LOOP

Autonomy you can actually trust.

Nexus operates with a philosophy of supervised autonomy — it reasons and recommends independently, but executes nothing irreversible without an explicit human gate. Every decision is explainable, every action is calibrated, and every change can be rolled back.

Explainable reasoning tracesEvery conclusion is accompanied by the evidence chain and reasoning path that produced it — no black boxes.
Calibrated confidence scoringAll findings carry a posterior confidence score. Low-confidence actions are automatically escalated for analyst review.
Supervised approval gatesDestructive or irreversible actions are held at a gate until an authorised analyst explicitly approves them.
Reversible by defaultEvery automated action has a corresponding rollback procedure verified before execution begins.

TRUST MATRIX

Controls built into every layer.

Scoped permissions & RBAC

Role-based access controls govern every agent capability. No lateral escalation is possible outside pre-defined permission scopes.

Hardware MFA Just-in-time access Session timeouts

Tamper-evident audit trail

Every platform action — by agents or analysts — is recorded in a cryptographically signed, append-only ledger that cannot be altered after the fact.

Signed ledger WORM storage SIEM forwarding

End-to-end encryption

All data in transit is protected with TLS 1.3. All data at rest uses AES-256-GCM. Customers may bring their own encryption keys at the Enterprise tier.

TLS 1.3 AES-256-GCM BYOK ready

Tenant isolation

Every environment is deployed in a fully isolated VPC with per-tenant encryption keys. Memory, agent state, and telemetry are sandboxed absolutely.

VPC isolation Per-tenant keys Memory sandboxing

Full explainability

Every agent decision is accompanied by a structured reasoning trace — which signals were used, how they were weighted, and why the conclusion was reached.

Reasoning traces Evidence chain Confidence scores

Before any response action executes, the blast radius is calculated and rollback procedures are verified. A platform-level kill switch halts all autonomous actions instantly.

AUDIT TRAIL

A signed record of every decision.

Regulators, auditors, and incident reviewers need to know not just what happened — but who decided it, when, and why. Nexus provides a tamper-proof answer to all three questions for every action it takes or recommends.

Immutable WORM ledgerEvery entry is cryptographically chained. No record can be deleted, modified, or back-dated after it is written.
Attributed to a person or agentEach action record carries a principal — either a named analyst who approved it, or the specific agent that triggered it.
Streamed to your SIEMThe audit ledger forwards in real time to your existing SIEM — Splunk, Sentinel, QRadar, or any CEF-compatible target.
