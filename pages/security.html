Security & trust

Autonomous, but accountable.

Nexus investigates on its own, and explains every step. Humans hold the keys to anything that changes your environment, behind approval gates, scoped permissions, and a tamper-evident audit trail.

Human-in-the-loop

Autonomy you can actually trust.

Nexus is autonomous in investigation and supervised in action. Every reasoning step is recorded, every verdict is scored, and anything destructive waits for a person to sign off.

  • Explainable reasoning tracesEvery hypothesis, the evidence behind it, and the verdict are logged in plain, reviewable steps.
  • Calibrated confidence scoringEach automated verdict carries a confidence score, low-confidence calls escalate instead of acting.
  • Supervised approval gatesDestructive actions block pending a single-click, cryptographically signed approval from authorised staff.
  • Reversible by defaultEvery action is rollback-verified before it runs, so automation never breaks production.

Audit trail

A signed record of every decision.

Nothing happens off the record. From the first signal to the final containment step, Nexus writes a cryptographically signed, tamper-evident ledger entry, forensically defensible and ready for any auditor.

  • Immutable WORM ledgerWrite-once, read-many storage means entries can be verified but never quietly altered.
  • Attributed to a person or agentEach entry names the analyst or agent responsible, with the signing key that authorised it.
  • Streamed to your SIEMForward the full trail into Splunk, Sentinel, or your data lake in real time.
