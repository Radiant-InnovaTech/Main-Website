About Me

Student. Builder.
Security Researcher.

I'm Samuel Nana Kweku Abekah Maclar — a final-year BSc Cybersecurity student at the University of Mines and Technology, Tarkwa, and co-founder & CEO of Radiant InnovaTech.

I work at the intersection of cybersecurity and AI. While studying how systems are attacked, I'm building the tools that defend them — from ML-powered threat detection to LLM-driven security assistants tuned for African threat landscapes.

My current focus is CyberSentinel: Africa's AI-powered SOC co-pilot, built to give African organisations enterprise-grade security intelligence at SME-accessible pricing. Alongside that, I'm completing my Final Year Project — a STRIDE-based conversational threat modelling assistant called GRC Auditor.
